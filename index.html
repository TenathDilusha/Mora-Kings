About Mora Chess

Mora Chess represents the University of Moratuwa in inter-university and national chess competitions. We are a passionate community dedicated to excellence in the royal game.

Founded over 25 years ago, we have grown into one of the most formidable university chess teams in Sri Lanka, nurturing talent and fostering a culture of strategic thinking.
