Who Are We

About Mora Chess

Mora Chess is the official chess team of the University of Moratuwa, bringing together strategic minds, competitive spirits, and a passion for the game of kings. We represent the university at inter-university, national, and international chess events while fostering a strong chess culture within the campus.

Throughout the years, we have grown into one of the most formidable university chess teams in Sri Lanka, nurturing talent and fostering a culture of strategic thinking.

Our Mission

To promote chess culture within the University of Moratuwa

To develop competitive players through training and mentorship

To represent the university with excellence and sportsmanship

To create a supportive and inclusive chess community
