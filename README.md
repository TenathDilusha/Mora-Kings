# ♟️Mora Chess Website

Official one-page website for **Mora Chess**, representing the **University of Moratuwa Chess Team**.

This project showcases the team’s history, achievements, events, and leadership, and serves as an online presence for chess-related activities at the University of Moratuwa.

---

## 🏫 About Mora Chess
Mora Chess is the official chess team of the **University of Moratuwa**, one of Sri Lanka’s leading technological universities.  
The team actively participates in inter-university, national, and international chess tournaments, promoting strategic thinking and sportsmanship among students.
